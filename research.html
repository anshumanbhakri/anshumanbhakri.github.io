Research Projects

Project 1: Contract Design in Renewable Energy Procurement Auctions

This research investigates how contract designs in the renewable energy sector influence procurement costs and deployment outcomes. By analyzing auction and post-award deployment data from the Indian solar energy sector, we assess the impact of bid indexing and penalties for non-deployment.

Read the paper

Project 2: Auctions under Uncertainty: A Model of Bidding and Deployment

This project develops a dynamic model of bidding in sequential auctions with uncertain post-award outcomes. The study focuses on how firms' private cost signals and uncertainty over future costs affect both bidding strategies and project deployment decisions.

Read the paper

Project 3: The Role of Penalties in Auction Design: Evidence from Solar Energy Auctions

This paper explores the role of penalties in auction design for renewable energy projects. We analyze how varying penalty structures influence bidding behavior and project completion rates in solar energy auctions.
