Contract Design in Renewable Energy Procurement Auctions

This research investigates how contract designs in the renewable energy sector influence procurement costs and deployment outcomes. By analyzing auction and post-award deployment data from the Indian solar energy sector, we assess the impact of bid indexing and penalties for non-deployment.

How large are the cost savings from auctions vs feed-in tariffs: Evidence from Germany

This project develops a dynamic bidding model in sequential auctions with uncertain post-award outcomes. The study focuses on how firms' private cost signals and uncertainty over future costs affect both bidding strategies and project deployment decisions.
