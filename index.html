Research

Project 1: Optimal Bidding Strategies in Sequential Auctions

This research explores the design of optimal bidding strategies in sequential auctions with a focus on the role of future uncertainties in procurement contracts. We use a dynamic model to simulate firm behavior and analyze the impact of indexing on bidding decisions.

Project 2: Contract Design and Deployment in the Solar Energy Sector

In this project, we study the design of contracts for large-scale solar projects, focusing on how to incentivize timely deployment under cost uncertainties. The research incorporates data from Indian solar auctions and analyzes various contract structures, including penalty clauses.
